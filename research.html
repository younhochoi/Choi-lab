Our Research Areas

• Viral Pathogenesis

Study of viral mechanisms, host interactions, and immune evasion, especially viral nonstructural protein effects.

• RNA Modification and Antiviral Response

Exploring how RNA modification pathways control viral replication and how viruses evade.

• Reverse Genetics of Bunyaviruses

Generation of mutant viruses to study pathogenesis and host–virus interactions.

• Animal Models

Using mouse and ferret models to investigate viral infection and host immune responses.
